In this exercise you have a Dynamic of pairs, and the goal is to create a pair of Dynamics and to make sure that neither of the Dynamics has any spurious updates.

You should fill out this function:

uniqueExercise :: (Reflex t, MonadFix m, MonadHold t m)
               => Dynamic t (Int, Int)
               -> m (Dynamic t Int, Dynamic t Int)
uniqueExercise dIn =
  pure (fst <$> dIn, snd <$> dIn)

to make this happen.
